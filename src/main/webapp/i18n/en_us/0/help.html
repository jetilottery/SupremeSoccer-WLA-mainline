Supreme Football

Gooooaaaal! Lace up your cleats and step onto the field in Supreme Football! Reveal prize values beneath the jerseys in each row.

When 3 identical prize values appear in a row, you win! Collect footballs on the prize values and move the bonus goal meter to trigger the bonus round. Keep your nerve in the shootout as you reveal the shooting players to try and win one of five prize levels!

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

{RevealAll}

Press {REVEALALL} to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

To continue the game in manual play mode, press STOP.

How To Play

The object of Supreme Football is to find three identical prize values in a row or collect 3 footballs to trigger the bonus round.

Select each of the jersey symbol to reveal a prize value. Find 3 identical prize values in a row to win.

Prize values also contain 0, 1, or 2 footballs. Revealing 3 footballs awards a bonus round.

In the bonus round, choose from 10 targets to reveal football players or X’s. Each player revealed increases the value of the instant win bonus cup. Once 3 X’s are revealed, the bonus ends and any awarded prize values are added to the total win.

To complete play, select all jerseys or press {REVEALALL}, after which a message appears indicating the game’s outcome, and play ends.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
